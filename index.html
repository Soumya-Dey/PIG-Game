Help
  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls 2 dice as many times as he whishes
  • Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • If the player rolls a 1 then his ROUND score gets lost
  • The player can choose to 'Hold' to add ROUND score to GLOBAL score
  • The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
  • You can enter your choice of final score and then play with that score
